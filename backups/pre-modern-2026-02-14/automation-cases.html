JAIBOT — Personal AI Assistant

JAIBOT is a personal AI assistant designed with a dual purpose. On one side, it works as an interactive way for people to learn about me: my background, experience and projects, through natural language. On the other, it acts as my own personal assistant, capable of supporting different tasks and workflows. The core logic is orchestrated using n8n, enabling modular, extensible automation flows that can evolve over time.

JAIBOT Art — Automated Content Creation

JAIBOT Art is an end-to-end automation project focused on content creation for social media. Starting from a selected theme, the system automatically gathers and processes information, generates visual content and written copy, and publishes the final result on Instagram. The entire pipeline is fully automated, combining AI models, creative tooling and workflow orchestration to transform ideas into published content with minimal manual intervention.
